CREEPYPASTA: "Call from 3:00 AM"

I woke up abruptly. My phone was vibrating incessantly, and in the dark room, the only light was the flashing screen. It was 3:00 AM. Exactly.

3:00 AM

A time I always knew was sinister. But I never believed it. Until that night.

My phone was going crazy. I had 49 missed calls in a matter of minutes. All were from unknown numbers, with prefixes I'd never seen before. Some seemed to be Italian, others... not. One seemed to be from the Vatican City, another from North Korea. One, in fact, had only three digits: 666.

As I watched, paralyzed by sleep and fear, a text arrived.

"You're awake. Now look."

And immediately after... a photo.

A figure. Half-naked. Ambiguous. A femboy, apparently, with a glassy gaze and an unnatural, almost forced pose. The background looked like a white, sterile room, without doors or windows.

But what made my blood run cold... was that behind him was a framed photo of myself hanging on the wall. Me... sleeping. That photo didn't exist. It couldn't exist.

I tried calling the police, but every time I dialed, the call would cut off, replaced by a metallic, distorted voice:

"You can't leave here. The algorithm has chosen you."

THE FIRST NIGHT

I thought it was a prank. Maybe a hacker attack, some strange virus. I turned off the phone and left it in the next room. But... at 3:00 AM sharp, every night, it all started all over again.

Calls. Photos. Videos.

In some videos, the figure wasn't alone. Other "femboys," with deformed, unrealistic features, appeared. Their faces were glitched, sometimes pixelated, sometimes… they seemed to have been cut from other photos, clumsily pasted together. Some laughed silently, others cried black, slimy tears.

Once I received an audio note. Just white noise. Then a whispered voice:

“You're only awake because they're watching you.”

THE DESCENT

I started doing research. I asked obscure forums, deleted subreddits, and even the deep web. I found a name: The 3 AM Loop.

An urban legend. A digital entity. No one knows who created it. Is it a virus? A digital ghost? Or something worse?

All the witnesses said the same thing: you receive photos of dead-eyed femboys, from unknown numbers, every night at 3:00 AM. If you answer… you disappear. One of them left his last trace on Reddit:

“I'll answer tonight. I'm tired. I want to know.”

That profile disappeared a few minutes later. The entire account was deleted. No trace, not even in the archive backups.

THE PRESENCE

One night, I left my phone on with the camera facing my room, recording. At 2:59, everything was normal. But at 3:01, something changed.

A figure appeared. Sitting on the edge of the bed. A femboy, but his face was blurry, as if pixelated in real time. He moved jerkily, as if in a video loop. He slowly raised his hand… and pointed at the camera. Then he disappeared into thin air.

I rewatched the video at least twenty times. With each viewing… something changed. Sometimes there was a second person. Sometimes I wasn't in bed.

But the worst part? In one very brief shot, there was a mirror in the background, one I've never had… and in the mirror was a man wearing a pig mask, standing behind me.

THE TRUE NATURE

After weeks, I received one last message.

“Do you want to know what the Loop is?”

Followed by a link. An .onion site on the deep web.

When I entered it, only a black page, with a single file to download: femboy.mp4.

I played it.

A figure sitting, crying. It said:

“We were created as images. As you wish. Now we are hungry. You looked at us. Now they look at you.”

“Photos… are windows. Every time you open them, you let us in a little more.”

“You are the door. And this is the last night.”

THE LAST NIGHT

It's 2:57 AM as I write this.

I feel my phone vibrating, but I haven't charged it in days. It's dead. It should be dead.

3:00 AM

The lights go out. The screen lights up on its own.

There's a new image. I won't open it. I don't want to.

But I know what's inside.

The photo of myself… sitting in bed… with one of them behind me.

I can hear him breathing.

EPILOGUE

If you're reading this… you could be next.

Don't answer calls at 3:00 AM.
Don't open photos.
Don't look too long.

And above all…
never desire what you don't understand.

The 3 O'Clock Loop is real.
And once he sees you…
he'll never stop looking.
