I don't even know why I'm writing this. Maybe to free myself, maybe so someone knows what happened to me, in case there's no tomorrow for me.
It was a normal night, I was awake with my phone in my hand, like always. Then, at 3:03, it vibrated.
A notification.
There was no name, no number. Just a message:
"I see you."
I thought it was a bug, spam, or maybe a prank from some weird app. I opened the chat... but there was nothing. The space was empty, as if the message had disappeared.
I tried to go back to sleep, but after a few minutes... another vibration.
Another message.
"I'm at your door."
I couldn't breathe. I ran to check: the door was locked, everything was fine. But the phone kept vibrating, always with new messages.
"I'm in your room."
"I'm behind you."
I couldn't move. I plucked up the courage to take a screenshot, to test it. But the screenshot didn't show the message... only the internal camera, active. And in the reflection, behind my shoulder, a shadow with two white eyes.
I dropped the phone, but it vibrated again. I picked it up, shaking.
Last message:
"Now it's your turn."
I haven't been able to sleep since then. I still feel phantom vibrations, but there's nothing on the screen.
I don't know if I'll be around tomorrow.
If you get a notification at 3:03 AM too... don't open it.
